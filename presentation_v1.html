Core Element

The Apex Mark

Precision & Ascent

The Akro mark represents the pinnacle of scientific achievement. The sharp geometry evokes absolute precision, while the upward trajectory symbolizes advancement, superiority, and the rigorous standards of our research peptides.

  • Engineered symmetry for flawless scaling across digital and printed assets.
  • Contained within a perfect circle to represent wholeness and the complete research cycle.
  • Minimalist execution ensures immediate recognition without reliance on color.
